A Lovely Three-Level Chocolate Cake
Section What to Do / Use
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • half cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 180 Â°C
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs until smooth.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 25 min
Frosting & Decorations
  • Simple chocolate buttercream
  • Then top with:
    • Fresh strawberries
    • White chocolate
Note Let it rest for at least 10 minutes it makes slicing way easier .
